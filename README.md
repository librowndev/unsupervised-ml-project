## About This Assignment

In this assignment, you will apply the tools and techniques learned in this course to build and compare Unsupervised Learning models (clustering and/or dimensionality reduction) using a dataset you feel passionate about. The goal is not only to perform the analysis, but also to clearly communicate insightful and impactful findings to stakeholders.

Treat this as an opportunity to produce a stakeholder-ready report that highlights your analytical skills for a senior audience, such as a Chief Data Officer (CDO) or Head of Analytics.

## Sections Required in Your Report

- **Main Objective:** State the goal of the analysis, specify whether the approach focuses on clustering or dimensionality reduction, and explain the benefits to stakeholders/business.

- **Dataset Description:** Briefly describe the dataset, summarize key attributes, and explain what the analysis aims to accomplish.

- **Data Exploration & Preparation:** Summarize data exploration and actions taken for data cleaning and/or feature engineering.

- **Model Variations:** Train and summarize at least three variations of the chosen unsupervised approach (e.g., different clustering techniques).

- **Final Model Recommendation:** Recommend the best model and explain why it best fits the project objectives.

- **Key Findings & Insights:** Present the main insights and results in a clear, well-structured way.

- **Next Steps:** Suggest actionable next steps, such as revisiting the model, adding new features, or exploring alternative techniques.
